Keres, My Eternal Love

Keres, your name is a whisper of love,
A melody sent from the heavens above.
Like roses in bloom with petals so fair,
Your beauty and kindness dance in the air.

Your eyes shine brighter than the moonlit sea,
A love so deep, it sets my heart free.
In every moment, in every dream,
You are my forever, my endless theme.

With every petal, with every sigh,
I promise to love you till the stars fade from the sky.
